Jan Samadhan

It is a website/web portal which helps a commosn man to send complaints to respected department. And it is not like other complaint system where we can only write a complaint to a particular department and have no any type of status about complaints. Jan Samadhan can take complaints of every department like electricity,BMC(Bhopal Municipal Corporation) etc and send to respected department and give status of complaints like open,assigned,progress,closed,ignored and invalid.

Status of Complaints

  • Open
  • Assigned
  • Progress
  • Closed
  • Ignored
  • Invalid

Open

The complaints is submited successfully.

Assigned

The complaint is assigned to employee by the head of department.

Progress

The department is taking action on alloted issue.

Closed

The issue is solved with the 100% satisfaction of complainant.

Ignored

No action taken by the department of respected issues which is kept by complainant.

Invalid

Proper issue is not defined by the complainant and it may be a insufficient content for the issue.
